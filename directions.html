Driving to Garrison Golf Club

From Hotels
  • Head North on Westage Business Center Dr toward Merritt Blvd
  • Take 1st right onto W Merritt Blvd
  • Take 2nd right onto Route 9 South; continue for 11 miles
  • Turn right onto The Garrison's side entrance

From New York (via Palisades Parkway)
  • At end of George Washington Bridge, take Palisades Pkwy North
  • Take Palisades Pkwy to the end to Bear Mountain Bridge
  • Cross Bear Mountain Bridge, at the end take immediate left onto Route 9D North
  • Travel appx 5 miles (through two traffic ligts)
  • Turn right onto Snake Hill Road
  • Continue appx 1.5 miles then turn left onto The Garrison side entrance

From White Plains (via Taconic / Rt 9)
  • Take Route 287 West to Exit 3 (Sprain Brook Pkwy / Taconic Pkwy North)
  • Exit Taconic for Route 9A/100 North
  • Route 9A merges with Route 9 North; continue on Route 9 North
  • Just past Peekskill, follow signs for Route 9 North towards Fishkill (left at traffic light)
  • The Garrison's main entrance is appx 6.5 miles ahead on the left

Public Transport to Garrison Golf Club

Trains from New York
Trains run from Grand Central to Garrison, NY. If you plan to take the train please let Gopi and Eric know so we can arrange for a shuttle bus for transportation from the Garrison station to the wedding venue.
